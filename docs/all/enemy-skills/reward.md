- 1 Legendary Item (<img src="/icons/legendary-item.svg" alt="Legendary Item Icon" class="icon-svg" />)
- 5 [tenacity](/docs/all/glossary/tenacity),
- Advance [Health](/docs/all/stats/health) stat by 1 (if possible).
